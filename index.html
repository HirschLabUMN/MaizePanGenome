What is the PAN genome project?

This project is seeking to understand the functional consequences of genome content variation on phenotypic outcomes and how the origin of genome content variation impacts phenotypic outcomes

Project Overview

Project goal 1

Identify genome content variation between maize inbred lines using a combination of de novo genome assemblies and exome capture.

Project goal 2

Identify mechanistic signatures that elucidate the origin of genome content variation.

Project goal 3

Identify genome content variation associated with phenotypic and chemotypic traits and identify mechanistic origins enriched within classes of traits.

Project goal 4

Utilize a set of synergistic activities aimed at providing young women with mentoring and training opportunities to foster their interest and engagement in STEM disciplines, and contribute to the increased representation of women in STEM.
